Core Healthcare Solutions

AI-Powered & Open-Source Health Information Systems

From AI-driven clinical documentation to industry-leading open-source healthcare platforms — we build and implement solutions trusted by organizations worldwide.

AI Scribe NEW

AI-powered clinical documentation that converts doctor–patient conversations into structured SOAP notes, consultation summaries, and clinical insights in real time. Seamlessly integrates with any EMR — featuring secure patient consent, live recording or audio upload, advanced speech-to-text (OpenAI Whisper), and LLM-powered medical documentation. Reduces clinician documentation time and lets healthcare providers focus on patient care.

Learn More

Bahmni EMR

An easy-to-use hospital management and electronic medical records system. Combines clinical, diagnostic, and patient management information for effective patient care, from registration to discharge.

Learn More

OpenMRS

A collaborative open-source platform for medical records designed for resource-poor environments. Modular, scalable, and customizable without programming — supporting healthcare delivery in developing countries.

Learn More

OpenELIS

Enterprise-level laboratory information system for public health laboratories. Manages sample tracking, test workflows, quality control, and reporting — supporting best laboratory practices and accreditation.

Learn More

OpenHIM

Health Information Mediator providing an interoperability layer that connects disparate health systems. Enables secure data exchange between hospitals, labs, pharmacies, and health registries using HL7 and FHIR standards.
